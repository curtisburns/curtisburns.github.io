About

Hi, my name is Curtis and back in June I decided to make the leap from my job in finance into the world of web development. I enrolled on the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly in order to gain an understanding of the fundamentals in coding for me to build upon in my next career. Since then I have not looked back, and have been able to work on some great projects throughout the last few months. Feel free to check them out!

I am constantly aiming to improve my skills and I am currently looking for a job and mentor, so if you can help with either of these please don't hesitate to reach out!
